Last updated: 20 February 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Zoom Photography Studios Ltd ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use SideKick LB software ("the Software"). By using the Software, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 License Information

When you activate a license, we collect only:

  • Your GoHighLevel Location ID (used as your license identifier)
  • License activation status and expiry date

2.2 Payment Processing

We have no access to your payment card details. All payments are processed securely by LemonSqueezy, our payment provider.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

SideKick LB stores no personal data. The Software operates entirely locally on your computer. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your name, address, or personal details
  • Your client data, contact information, or booking details
  • Payment card numbers, bank details, or financial information
  • GoHighLevel API credentials or access tokens
  • Light Blue data or exports
  • Any data you enter into the Software

2.4 Local Data Storage

The Software stores only configuration settings locally on your computer in an INI file. No personal data is stored. This configuration data never leaves your machine.

3. Third-Party Services

The Software integrates with the following third-party services. Each service has its own privacy policy:

  • LemonSqueezy: Handles all subscription billing and payment processing.
  • GoHighLevel: CRM data is accessed via your own API credentials and processed locally on your computer
  • Light Blue: Studio management data is read locally — we never access your Light Blue account

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.
